Daily Specials Sonic Deals

Happy Hour

Sonic’s Happy Hour is a daily event that provides customers with fantastic savings on their favorite drinks and slushes. Here are the details:

  • Timing: Every day from 2 PM to 4 PM.
  • Discounts: During this time, all drinks and slushes are half-priced. This includes a variety of options like iced teas, soft drinks, lemonades, limeades, and the popular Ocean Water.
  • Extended Happy Hour: By ordering through the Sonic app, you can enjoy Happy Hour prices all day long.

Exclusive App and Text Deals

Sonic offers exclusive deals and promotions for customers who use their mobile app or sign up for text alerts. These sonic deals provide additional savings and convenience, making it easier for customers to enjoy their favorite Sonic menu items.

Sonic App

The Sonic app offers a range of benefits, including exclusive discounts, easy ordering, and personalized sonic deals. By downloading the app, customers can access app-only discounts and special offers, such as half-price drinks and slushes available all day.

Benefits of the Sonic App:

  • Easy Ordering: Order ahead and customize your meals with ease.

Text Alerts

By signing up for Sonic’s text alerts, customers can receive the latest deals and promotions directly to their phones. This ensures that they never miss out on a great offer.

How to Sign Up for Text Alerts:

  • Text “DRIVEIN” to 876642 to start receiving exclusive deals.
  • Receive notifications about new promotions, discounts, and limited-time offers.
